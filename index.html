Plants in Ayurveda

Tulsi

- Botanical Name: Ocimum sanctum
- Common Name: Tulsi, Holy Basil
- Habitat: Tropical and subtropical regions, commonly grown in homes and gardens in India.
- Uses: Respiratory disorders, fever, stress, immune booster.
- Disease and Cure: Treats coughs, colds, bronchitis, and asthma.

Neem

Botanical Name: Azadirachta indica
- Common Name: Neem, Indian Lilac
- Habitat:Indian subcontinent, tropical and semi-tropical regions.
- Uses: Anti-inflammatory, antifungal, antibacterial.
- Disease and Cure: Skin conditions like eczema, acne, psoriasis; dental care.

Brahmi

-Botanical Name: Bacopa monnieri
- Common Name: Brahmi
- Habitat: Wet, tropical environments in India, Nepal, and Sri Lanka.
- Uses: Cognitive enhancement, memory improvement, anxiety reduction.
- Disease and Cure: Treats cognitive disorders, Alzheimer's, ADHD.

Amla

- Botanical Name: Phyllanthus emblica
- Common Name: Amla, Indian Gooseberry
- Habitat: Tropical regions of India and Southeast Asia.
- Uses: Immune booster, digestion aid, hair health.
- Disease and Cure: Treats digestive issues, cold, and scurvy.

Turmeric

- Botanical Name: Curcuma longa
- Common Name: Turmeric, Haldi
- Habitat: Tropical regions of South Asia.
- Uses: Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, pain relief.
- Disease and Cure: Treats arthritis, digestive issues, and skin conditions.

Ginger

- Botanical Name: Zingiber officinale
- Common Name: Ginger, Adrak
- Habitat: Tropical and subtropical regions.
- Uses: Digestive aid, anti-nausea, anti-inflammatory.
- Disease and Cure:Treats indigestion, nausea, and joint pain.

Licorice

- Botanical Name: Glycyrrhiza glabra
- Common Name: Licorice, Mulethi
- Habitat: Subtropical regions of Europe and Asia.
- Uses: Respiratory support, digestive aid, anti-inflammatory.
- Disease and Cure: Treats sore throat, ulcers, and respiratory infections.

Shatavari

- Botanical Name: Asparagus racemosus
- Common Name: Shatavari
- Habitat: Found in tropical and subtropical regions of India.
- Uses:Female reproductive health, adaptogen, immune support.
- Disease and Cure: Treats PMS, menopause symptoms, and boosts immunity.

Giloy

- Botanical Name: Tinospora cordifolia
- Common Name: Giloy, Guduchi
- Habitat: Tropical regions of India.
- Uses:Immune booster, anti-inflammatory, antipyretic.
- Disease and Cure: Treats fever, diabetes, and respiratory issues.

Nirgundi

- Botanical Name: Vitex negundo
- Common Name: Nirgundi
- Habitat: Grows in moist areas, particularly in the Indian subcontinent.
- Uses: Anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and anti-arthritic.
- Disease and Cure:Treats joint pain, arthritis
